About Me

My journey into data began with earning a Data Analyst Certificate from Coursera, followed by an upskilling program in Data Analytics through the K-Youth Program with Excelerate Asia. Today, I’m a Business Intelligence Executive, passionate about turning raw data into meaningful insights that drive impactful decision-making.

While working as a Business Analyst, I discovered my passion for automating business processes. I’ve worked on transforming manual reporting tasks and tedious workflows into more efficient, automated systems. Whether it’s designing easy-to-use dashboards or integrating tools that reduce errors, I love finding ways to make everyday tasks simpler and less time-consuming.

For me, automation is about more than just getting things done faster. It’s about helping businesses free up time to focus on bigger-picture goals like strategy and growth.✨
